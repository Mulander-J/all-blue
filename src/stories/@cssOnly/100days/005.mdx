## Learn

Render chart by svg

### yAxis

A few horizontal straight lines with a spacing of 45

```html
<path d="M 0,0 L 266,0"></path>
<path d="M 0,45 L 266,45"></path>
<path d="M 0,90 L 266,90"></path>
```

### xAxis

On the x-axis, reserve 10 on the left and right, and take one point at intervals of 35

```html
<text x="28" y="110">Mon</text>
<text x="63" y="110">Tue</text>
<text x="98" y="110">Wen</text>
<!-- ... -->
```

### dot

Combine the x and y coordinates of the additional points

```html
<circle cx="28" cy="61" r="3px"></circle>
<circle cx="63" cy="20" r="3px"></circle>
<!-- ... -->
```

### line

Combine the x and y coordinates of the additional points to form a polyline

```html
<polyline
  width="288"
  points="28,61 63,20 98,65 133,55 168,61 203,74 238,64"
></polyline>
```
